    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are answers to common questions about our AI-powered medical diagnostic tools.

    What types of medical imaging does your AI analyze?

    Our AI tools analyze various medical imaging modalities, including MRI, X-ray, CT scans, endoscopy, and colonoscopy. Each tool is designed to detect specific conditions, such as tumors, fractures, infections, and early-stage cancers.

    How accurate is your AI in diagnosing medical conditions?

    Our AI achieves an accuracy rate of up to 98% in detecting abnormalities across different imaging modalities. This high precision helps reduce misdiagnosis and ensures reliable insights for healthcare professionals.

    Can your tools work offline in remote areas?

    Yes, our diagnostic tools are designed to function offline, making them ideal for remote clinics and areas with limited internet connectivity. This ensures uninterrupted access to advanced diagnostics wherever you are.

    Do you offer training for using your AI tools?

    Absolutely! We provide comprehensive training programs, user guides, and on-site sessions to ensure seamless adoption of our tools. Our support team is also available to assist with any questions.
